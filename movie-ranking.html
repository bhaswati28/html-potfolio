Top three must watch Movies

The Green Mile

Starring Michael Clarke and Tom Hanks, a tale set on death row in a Southern jail,where gentle giant John possesses the mysterious power to heal people's ailment.

1922

A farmer conspires to murder his wife for monetary gains. Wilfred and his son Henry soon become delusional as they continue with their lives.

Smile

A 2022 psychological horror based on a curse that brings people together.
